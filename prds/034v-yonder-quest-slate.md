---

## 1. Strategic Read

The quest layer should not be built around arbitrary completionism.

The strongest Yonder quests are the ones that:

- are easy to explain
- have strong visual or experiential payoff
- feel like a story, not a spreadsheet
- can be completed in a realistic geography or season

Based on the current expanded artifact slate, Yonder already has enough material to draft a real quest shortlist.

- `prds/034x-yonder-launch-artifact-slate.md`

## 3. Recommended Launch Five

If Yonder only launches `5` quests first, these are the strongest:

1. Hidden Waterfalls of North Georgia
2. Fire Towers and Summit Views
3. Secret Atlanta: Oddities and Outdoor Art
4. Chattahoochee Access Circuit
5. Starter Quest: Big Payoff, Low-Friction Outdoors

Why these five:

- they are easy to explain
- they cover both city and regional exploration
- they have strong visual payoff
- they do not all depend on hard-seasonality
- they create different entry points for different users
