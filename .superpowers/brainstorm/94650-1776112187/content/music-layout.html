B: Unified Timeline (Show-First)

Everything organized chronologically. Tonight's shows are elevated with images and full detail, this week's shows are compact rows. Venue is secondary metadata on each show.

Pros

  • Simple mental model — scroll = time
  • Easy to scan "what's next"
  • No two-zone complexity

Cons

  • Venues lose their identity as anchors
  • Bars-with-a-band creep back in (no venue tier separation)
  • Loses browse value for non-time-sensitive use
