Template-driven, data-backed. No AI generation. Three voice patterns:

Voice Patterns — assembled from real data, never invented

3. Emotional Moments

Belonging → Discovery → Urgency. Small design touches that create feeling.

Moment Design — micro-interactions that create connection
BELONGING
Friend Activity Pulse
• "Sarah just checked in at Ponce City Market"
• Friend avatars on event cards they're attending
• "Your crew's favorite spot" badge on regular venues
• Gentle nudge: "Mike is going tonight — join?"
DISCOVERY
Hidden Gems & Surprises
• "Only 3 events today at this gallery" rarity signal
• "New venue" badge for places open < 6 months
• "Wildcard" section — random high-quality event you wouldn't search for
• "First time on Lost City" badge for newly crawled events
URGENCY (light touch)
Time-Aware Signals
• "Starts in 2 hours" on tonight's events
• "Last night" for closing exhibitions
• "Selling fast" from ticket platform data
• Time-of-day transitions (afternoon → evening → late night)
CRAFT
Motion & Polish
• Staggered card enter animations
• Save bookmark animation (heart fills)
• Smooth page transitions between feed → detail
• Pull-to-refresh with city skyline animation

What makes this "elite" vs. "good"

Good: Consistent tokens, matching components, proper spacing. (What we'd get from just fixing drift.)
Elite: The design communicates. Big things look big. The city has a voice. Your friends are woven in. You discover things you didn't know existed. The app makes you want to go out — not just know what's out there.
