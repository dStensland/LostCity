Visual signature per entry type

RSVP (coral)
Your commitment to a single event. Friend avatars stack right when shared.
Series (sage)
"Weekly" pill. Same visual weight as RSVP — it IS an RSVP, just recurring.
Plan (gold)
Expandable. Collapsed: "3 stops · 6pm – late." Tap to see stops. Participant avatars.
Friend (blue, dimmer)
70% opacity. Below your entries for that day. Tap → event detail → RSVP yourself.
Gap (dashed, warm)
Dashed border, sparkle icon. "Friday is open → Explore." Links to /explore?date=2026-04-17.
