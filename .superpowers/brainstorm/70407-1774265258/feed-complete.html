4

WORTH CHECKING OUT — Contextual Destinations

What it shows: 4-6 venue cards that respond to context — time of day, weather, day of week, occasion. Monday morning → coffee spots. Friday evening → date night restaurants. Rainy Saturday → museums and galleries.

Data sources (all exist): venue_occasions (4,431 rows), editorial_mentions (193 rows), hours (open now filter), weather API, time of day.

Editorial voice: Each card shows an occasion tag ("OPEN NOW", "PERFECT FOR DATE NIGHT", "RAINY DAY PICK") plus press quote if available.

Position: After The Lineup, before news. Answers "where should I go?" after "what's happening?"

8

AROUND THE CITY — Portal Teasers

What it shows: One headline card per active portal — the most relevant thing happening in each vertical. Civic: next council meeting. Arts: new exhibitions this week. Family: school calendar event. Adventure: trail conditions.

Design: Each card branded with portal accent color (teal for Citizen, copper for Arts, sage for Family). Left accent border matches portal identity. Compact — title + one line context + "See details →" link.

Position: Below the fold, after Regular Hangs + Now Showing. For users who scroll deep — "here's what else is going on in Atlanta beyond events."

Implementation: Each portal exposes a "headline" endpoint returning its top item. The Atlanta feed fetches these in parallel. Degrades gracefully — if a portal has nothing notable, its card doesn't show.
