Sidebar Identity Zones

Each entity type owns its identity zone — the sidebar content between hero and actions. This is where types genuinely differ.

Shared Sidebar Structure

Sidebar Skeleton (shared)
HeroZone
config: aspect, fallback, gallery
IdentityZone
ReactNode — per-type
ActionZone
config: primaryCTA, secondaries
