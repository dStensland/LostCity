Key design decisions

News is context, not the main event. 3 headlines, no tabs at top level, culture-first. "All news →" drills into the full TodayInAtlantaSection with category tabs.
Signal strip is conditional. Pollen only shows at code orange+. Moon only on notable phases (full, new). Sports only on game days. Not every pill every day.
The briefing zone ends with a clear boundary. "THE LINEUP ↓" marks the transition from context → action. Above is table-setting. Below is deciding what to do.
One unified zone, not stacked sections. Hero image + signals + headline + news are ONE visual area with a gradient treatment, not separate boxes.
