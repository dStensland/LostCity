# City Portals Visual Comparison

## Overview
Three city-type portals in the Atlanta metro area, each with distinct cultural identity and visual branding.

---

## Marietta: Corporate Professional

**Identity**: Historic downtown city with vibrant arts scene
**Vibe**: Professional, established, corporate
**Visual Preset**: `corporate_clean`

### Colors
- Primary: `#2563eb` (Blue) - Professional, trustworthy
- Secondary: `#7c3aed` (Purple) - Creative sophistication
- Accent: `#dc2626` (Red) - Energy, importance
- Background: `#f8fafc` (Light Slate) - Clean, modern
- Card: `#ffffff` (White) - Crisp, professional

### Style
- Border Radius: `md` (moderate, professional)
- Shadows: `medium`
- Card Style: `elevated`
- Glow: **Disabled** (clean, no-nonsense)
- Glass: **Disabled**
- Animations: `subtle`

### Ambient
- Effect: `subtle_glow`
- Intensity: `subtle`
- Colors: Light blue, light purple
- Speed: `slow`

**Feel**: Professional conference, corporate event, established institution

## Design Rationale

### Why Different Colors?

**Marietta (Blue/Purple)**
- Corporate = Professional = Blue
- Arts scene = Creative = Purple accent
- Clean, trustworthy, established

**Decatur (Orange/Brown)**
- Progressive = Energetic = Orange
- Walkable community = Earthy = Brown
- Artsy, eclectic, friendly

**College Park (Red/Gold)**
- Black culture = Rich warmth = Red
- Historic significance = Heritage = Gold
- Gullah-Geechee = Deep roots = Earth tones

### Why Different Intensities?

**Marietta**: Subtle (professional restraint)
**Decatur**: Medium (creative energy)
**College Park**: Medium (cultural warmth without overwhelming)

### Why Different Presets?

Each preset reflects the community's **core identity**:
- `corporate_clean` = Business-friendly, reliable
- `warm_creative` = Artistic, welcoming
- `soulful_heritage` = Cultural, historic
