The Opportunity

Hospitals and cultural institutions sit at the center of their communities — but their digital presence stops at the front door.

The problem

  • Patients and visitors have no way to find nearby food, lodging, pharmacies, or support services from the hospital
  • Community health organizations are invisible — buried across dozens of disconnected websites
  • Event and program data is fragmented, stale, and impossible to discover
  • Hospitals spend millions on patient experience but nothing on the community layer
  • Cultural institutions compete for attention with no shared infrastructure

Our solution

  • White-labeled community portals that extend any institution's brand into the surrounding neighborhood
  • Real-time event and resource feeds powered by an automated crawler network
  • 200+ curated community organizations with verified support tracks
  • Context-aware concierge that adapts to visitor type — patient, caregiver, staff, or urgent
  • Deployable in weeks, not months — one platform, infinite portals

How It Works

From crawl to concierge in minutes

Our automated pipeline continuously discovers, enriches, and surfaces community resources.

1

Crawl & ingest

50+ automated crawlers pull events, exhibits, programs, and services from venues, museums, organizations, and public health agencies across the metro area. New sources are added in hours, not weeks.

2

Enrich & classify

Events are deduplicated, geocoded, tagged by category, and scored for relevance. Support organizations are mapped against 32 verified tracks covering food access, mental health, housing, childcare, and more.

3

Brand & deploy

Each portal gets a fully white-labeled experience with custom themes, fonts, colors, and navigation. Hospital portals include campus-specific concierge, community hub, and support resource directory.

4

Adapt & personalize

The UI adapts in real time to context: visitor vs. patient vs. staff, time of day, campus location, and urgency mode. Open/closed status, late-night resources, and seasonal tips surface automatically.

Architecture

Modern stack, production-ready

Enterprise-grade infrastructure with zero client-side complexity.

Next.js 16

App Router with server components, streaming, and edge rendering. Sub-second page loads with zero client JavaScript on first paint.

Supabase

Postgres database with Row Level Security, real-time subscriptions, and edge functions. Battle-tested auth and storage.

Mapbox

Interactive maps with clustering, geocoding, and custom light-theme styles. Venue discovery with distance-aware ranking.

Crawler Network

50+ TypeScript crawlers running on scheduled intervals. Each crawler handles a specific venue, museum, or organization with full error recovery.

White-Label Theming

CSS variable-driven theming with visual presets, custom fonts, category colors, and component-level style overrides — all from the database.

i18n & Analytics

Multi-language support with next-intl. Portal-scoped analytics tracking every interaction — wayfinding, resource clicks, mode switches, and more.

Case Study

Emory Healthcare demo

A fully functional hospital community portal covering four Emory campuses in metro Atlanta.

🏥

Campus Concierge

Each hospital gets its own landing page with hero photo, address, wayfinding, and campus-specific resources. Resources show real-time open/closed status and are grouped by audience (patient, visitor, caregiver, staff).

🍕

Nearby Explorer

Interactive food, lodging, essentials, fitness, and escapes explorer with time-of-day awareness. Filters for breakfast/lunch/dinner/late-night and dietary preferences. Distance-ranked with open-now indicators.

🤝

Community Hub

Aggregates health programs, support groups, food pantries, fitness classes, and community events from across the metro. Category pathways with org counts. Discovery deck with search, filters, sort, map view, and timeline.

🛡

Support Resource Directory

200+ verified organizations across 32 support tracks: food access, mental health, housing, childcare, substance use recovery, domestic violence, elder care, financial assistance, legal aid, and more.

🔄

Audience Modes

Four intelligent modes — Visiting, Treatment, Urgent, and Staff — that reshape the entire experience. Different resources, tips, and urgency levels surface based on who you are.
