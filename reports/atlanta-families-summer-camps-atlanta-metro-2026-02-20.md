# Atlanta Families: Summer Camps Research (Atlanta Metro)

Date: 2026-02-20
Portal default: `atlanta-families`
Surface scope: `consumer` research input (with admin-grade attribution fields retained in CSV)

## Coverage and Method

- Primary source: Atlanta Parent 2026 summer camp guide pages (day camps + overnight camps).
- Metro filter: all day-camp entries (the source page is explicitly Metro Atlanta) plus only overnight entries explicitly mentioning Atlanta-metro locations in listing blurbs.
- De-duplication: normalized by camp name across featured/category repeats.
- Output count: **127 unique camps** from **132 source rows**.

## Source Attribution Contract

- Tier: Tier 1 discovery source (regional family publisher directory/guide).
- Required follow-up before production ingestion: verify each camp against official organizer URL and current session dates/ages/pricing.
- Provenance fields in CSV: `source_name`, `listing_url`, `research_date`, `section`, `blurb`.
