# Neon Readability

Neon Readability keeps the nightlife personality intact while moving legibility from fragile to reliable. Atmosphere stays loud through grain, electric color, and cinematic modules, but text sits on stronger scrims and cleaner panels so scanning remains fast at any brightness level.

Hierarchy is sharpened without flattening expression. The hero still performs as a statement piece, while event modules keep varied visual character instead of collapsing into one generic card style. Consistency appears in metadata order and spacing rhythm, not in visual sameness.

Accent color is treated as guidance. Pink, cyan, gold, and green are used as functional cues on chips, tags, and status lines, preserving the original energy while reducing decorative clutter. Each section should feel crafted and distinctive, yet instantly readable.

The goal is personality-first utility: a feed that still feels like Atlanta at night, but supports rapid decision-making with higher contrast text, calmer dense areas, and deliberate breathing room between modules.
